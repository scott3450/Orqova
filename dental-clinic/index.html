The Patient Lifecycle Framework

Patient Lifecycle Optimization

Dentists operate in a high-margin, high-trust environment. Orqova provides a HIPAA-compliant infrastructure to automate lead capture, post-op instructions, and reputation management—without losing the professional touch.

Core Mechanics

  • 1. Intelligent Lead Capture

    24/7 AI-driven chatbot for triage and insurance qualification before they ever speak to a receptionist.

  • 2. Post-Operative Follow-up

    Automated care sequences and check-ins 24 hours post-procedure, reducing manual phone time for staff.

  • 3. Review Generation (Smile Surveys)

    Positive feedbacks are funneled to Google Maps. Negative ratings are kept private for management review.
