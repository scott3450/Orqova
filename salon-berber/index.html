The Chair Occupancy Framework

Chair Occupancy & Churn Prevention

Barbers operate on high-frequency, low-margin transactions. Orqova focuses entirely on Leakage Prevention, ensuring your chairs stay occupied and product stock is precisely managed.

Core Mechanics

  • 1. No-Show Automation

    Implement a double-tap SMS confirmation system. If a client doesn't confirm 2 hours prior, the slot is automatically opened to a 'waitlist' via automated blast.

  • 2. Churn Recovery

    Automated triggers for clients who haven't booked in 4+ weeks, offering a 'Welcome Back' discount or loyalty incentive.

  • 3. Predictive Inventory Automation

    A simple tracking system that alerts the owner when product stock (gels, blades, aftershave) hits a critical threshold.
